How much does a portable toilet rental cost nationwide?

Pricing depends on unit type, rental length, servicing frequency, and location. Standard units cost less than luxury trailers.

The number depends on guest count, event duration, and whether food or alcohol is served.

Yes. Scheduled servicing includes waste removal, cleaning, deodorizing, and restocking supplies.

Can I rent portable toilets for construction sites?

Absolutely. Construction site porta potty rentals are one of our most common services.
